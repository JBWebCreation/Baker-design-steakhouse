About us

In 2009, Mark and Jennie Westinghouse had a dream. Their food had become a neighborhood favorite. They used only premium ingredients and were constantly having company over at their home in western Montana. Mark loved grilling for his friends and family and Jennie loved serving her signature mashed potatoes and entertaining their guests. But they wanted to make their meals something more.

In 2011, their dream had come true. They sold their home in Montana and moved to northern Idaho and started Baker Design Steakhouse. They now serve over one million customers per year. Now their steakhouse is expanding to more locations across both Idaho and Montana.

Come check out what has made their steakhouse a household name across the inland northwest.
